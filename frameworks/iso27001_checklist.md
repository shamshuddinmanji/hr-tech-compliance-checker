# ISO 27001 Compliance Checklist for HR Tech Vendors

> Use this checklist when assessing an HR Technology vendor against the ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management Standard.
> Score each item and calculate the overall vendor risk score on a 0–5 scale.

---

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Risk Level | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Low Risk | Fully Compliant |
| 4 | Low-Medium | Minor Gaps |
| 3 | Medium | Notable Gaps |
| 2 | High | Significant Gaps |
| 1 | Critical | Major Non-Compliance |
| 0 | Unacceptable | Do Not Proceed |

---

## Section 1 — Information Security Policy (A.5)

- [ ] A formal information security policy exists and is approved by management
- [ ] The policy is reviewed at planned intervals or when significant changes occur
- [ ] The policy is communicated to all employees and relevant third parties

---

## Section 2 — Organisation of Information Security (A.6)

- [ ] Information security roles and responsibilities are clearly defined
- [ ] Segregation of duties is applied to reduce risk of unauthorised access
- [ ] Security requirements are addressed in agreements with third parties

---

## Section 3 — Human Resource Security (A.7)

- [ ] Background verification checks are conducted on employees handling sensitive data
- [ ] Security awareness training is provided to all staff
- [ ] Employees are required to acknowledge and comply with security policies
- [ ] Clear offboarding procedures exist to revoke access upon termination

---

## Section 4 — Asset Management (A.8)

- [ ] An inventory of information assets is maintained
- [ ] Data classification policy is in place (e.g. Confidential, Internal, Public)
- [ ] Acceptable use policy for information assets is documented
- [ ] Secure disposal of media and equipment is practiced

---

## Section 5 — Access Control (A.9)

- [ ] Access control policy is documented and enforced
- [ ] Access is granted based on least privilege and need-to-know principles
- [ ] User access reviews are conducted periodically
- [ ] Privileged access is managed and monitored separately
- [ ] Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is implemented for critical systems

---

## Section 6 — Cryptography (A.10)

- [ ] A cryptography policy is in place
- [ ] Encryption is applied to sensitive data at rest and in transit
- [ ] Encryption key management procedures are documented

---

## Section 7 — Physical & Environmental Security (A.11)

- [ ] Physical access to data centres and offices is controlled
- [ ] Equipment is protected from physical threats and environmental hazards
- [ ] Clear desk and clear screen policies are enforced

---

## Section 8 — Operations Security (A.12)

- [ ] Operating procedures are documented and available
- [ ] Change management process is in place for IT systems
- [ ] Malware protection controls are implemented
- [ ] System logs are collected, monitored, and protected
- [ ] Vulnerability assessments are conducted regularly

---

## Section 9 — Communications Security (A.13)

- [ ] Network segmentation is in place to protect sensitive data
- [ ] Data transfer policies cover both internal and external transfers
- [ ] Confidentiality agreements cover electronic communications

---

## Section 10 — Supplier Relationships (A.15)

- [ ] Information security requirements are agreed with all suppliers
- [ ] Supplier agreements include data protection and security obligations
- [ ] Supplier compliance is monitored and reviewed regularly
- [ ] Vendor maintains a register of critical suppliers

---

## Section 11 — Information Security Incident Management (A.16)

- [ ] Incident management policy and procedures are documented
- [ ] Security events are reported and assessed in a timely manner
- [ ] Lessons learned from incidents are used to improve controls
- [ ] Incident response team and escalation paths are defined

---

## Section 12 — Business Continuity Management (A.17)

- [ ] Business continuity plan (BCP) includes information security
- [ ] Recovery time objectives (RTO) and recovery point objectives (RPO) are defined
- [ ] BCP is tested and reviewed at planned intervals

---

## Section 13 — Compliance (A.18)

- [ ] Vendor identifies and documents applicable legal and regulatory requirements
- [ ] Intellectual property rights and data protection laws are complied with
- [ ] ISO 27001 certification is current and available upon request
- [ ] Internal audits of the ISMS are conducted regularly
